Core Services

Irrigation Systems Design

Centre pivot and drip irrigation design, hydraulic calculations, layout, filtration and fertigation planning, performance evaluation and retrofits.

Survey Control & Topographic Mapping

GNSS/RTK control, total station surveys, stakeout and as-built surveys to ensure precise installation and reliable mapping.

GIS Services

Land-use mapping, beacon relocation planning and GIS-based irrigation zoning. Deliverables include shapefiles and printable maps.

Project Support

CAD drawings, BoQs, procurement support, installation supervision, commissioning, operator training and ongoing technical support.

Project Workflow

  1. Initial consultation & site assessment
  2. Control survey and topographic mapping
  3. Design development and client review
  4. Procurement & installation support
  5. Commissioning, training & maintenance handover
